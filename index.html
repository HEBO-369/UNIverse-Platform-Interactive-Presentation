UNIverse Platform

Integrated University Management System - HCI Fall 2025

System Architecture

A relational design ensuring data integrity and efficient management

User Management

  • Unified Access: Secure login for all roles.
  • Faculty Data: Doctors & Teaching Assistants.
  • Student Records: GPA, Levels, and Groups.

Academic Structure

  • Course Catalog: Departments and subjects.
  • Enrollments: Managing student registrations.
  • Scheduling: Timetables and slot allocation.

Activities Tracking

  • Assessments: Assignments and deadlines.
  • Performance: Grading and submission tracking.
  • Resources: Lecture notes and materials.

Authentication & Security

Secure access control using session based authentication

Unified Login

A single entry point for all users. The system identifies the user's role dynamically and redirects them to their personalized dashboard.

Easy Registration

Dedicated registration portals ensure accurate data collection. Students provide academic details like their department and group during sign-up.

Supervisor Module

Infrastructure & Administration Management

01

System Dashboard

Provides a high-level statistical overview of the system. It displays the total count of registered doctors, students, and active courses.

02

Available Courses

The supervisor defines which courses are offered in the current semester. This toggles the visibility of courses for student registration.

03

Generate Timetable

An automated tool to assign time slots to courses for different student groups, preventing time conflicts.

04

Manage Faculty

Allows the supervisor to register new Doctors and Teaching Assistants. The system generates a default password and assigns the appropriate role.

Academic Staff Module

Content Delivery & Student Assessment

01

Doctor Dashboard

A personalized landing page listing the courses assigned to the doctor. It acts as a gateway to manage each course.

02

Course Details

The central hub for a specific course. It displays metadata (code, credit hours) and lists all enrolled students.

03

Upload Material

Allows the doctor to upload lectures, labs, and supplementary PDFs for students.

04

Add Assignment

Doctors can create new tasks with specific deadlines and descriptions.

05

View Submissions

A view to see which students have submitted their work, download their files, and grade them.

06

View Announcements

Doctors can view and manage the history of announcements sent to students in this course.

Student Module

Learning & Progress Tracking

01

Student Dashboard

Shows the student's current enrollment status and quick links to their active courses.

02

My Courses

Displays a grid of courses the student is registered in. Clicking a course navigates to its specific materials and tasks.

03

View Schedule

Displays the weekly timetable for the student's specific group (lectures and labs).

04

Submit Assignments

An interface to view pending assignments and upload solutions.

05

View Grades

A gradebook view showing marks received for various assignments and exams.

06

View Material

A repository view where students can download PDFs and slides uploaded by the doctor.

07

View Announcements

A feed of updates sent by course instructors regarding exams or lectures.

08

Questions (Q&A)

A forum-like feature allowing students to ask questions to the staff and view answers.

Conclusion

Final Remarks & Project Outcome

Phase 4 of the UNIverse project successfully integrated the backend logic with the frontend interfaces. The database design supports strict data integrity across all modules. By separating the interfaces for Supervisors, Doctors, and Students, the system ensures a secure and optimized user experience for each role, fulfilling the academic management requirements effectively.
